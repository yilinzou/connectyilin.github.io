Yilin Zou

To the Oak Tree

By Shu Ting

If I love you
I will never be a clinging trumpet creeper
Using your high boughs to show off my height
If I love you
I will never be a spoony bird
Repeating a monotonous song for green shade
Or be a spring
Bring cool solace all year long
Or be a steep peak
Increasing your stature, reflecting your eminence
Even the sunlight, even the spring rain
No, all these are not enough NEXT PAGE
